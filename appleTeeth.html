Halloween Fruit Apple Teeth Treats



"These are absolutely adorable, pretty easy to make, and the perfect treat to bring to a Halloween party. I brought them to my 2-year-old's school party and they LOVED them, and so did the moms! Use organic apples if possible."

--Jen

Ingredients

  • 4 Honeycrisp apples - cored and quartered
  • 1 package blanched slivered almonds
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. With a small, sharp paring knife, cut a lengthwise wedge from the skin side of each apple quarter, leaving the peel around the wedge for lips.
  2. If desired, rub the cut portions of the apple quarters with lemon juice to prevent browning.
  3. Poke 5 or 6 slivered almonds into the top and bottom of the cut-out area to make snaggly teeth.
